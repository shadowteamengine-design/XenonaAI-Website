Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers

Do I need an API key?

No! XenonaAI runs entirely on your own computer with your own trained model. No API keys, no subscriptions, no external services required.

Do I need to install anything else?

No! XenonaAI comes as a standalone application with everything bundled. Just download, install, and run - no additional software needed.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! Your conversations and AI model run 100% locally on your computer. Nothing is sent to external servers - your data never leaves your device.

Can I customize the AI's behavior?

Yes! You can train your own custom models on different datasets, adjust temperature settings, and personalize the interface theme to match your preferences.

How much does it cost?

Completely free and open source! This is a learning project with zero budget - just someone learning to build AI from scratch and sharing the journey.

What can I realistically expect?

Honest answer: It's a 25M parameter model, not GPT-4. It can do basic text completion and simple conversations, but won't rival ChatGPT. Think of it as a learning experiment - you'll see how AI works from the ground up!

Will there be updates?

Yes! As I learn more, I'll improve the model and add features. Follow the GitHub repo to see the progress!
